
# Tightrope Game

**Tightrope Game** is a unique daily trivia platform where your knowledge is your only safety net. Challenge yourself with diverse questions, maintain your balance, and see how far you can go!

* **Web Version**: [tightropegame.online](https://tightropegame.online)

* **Balance Mechanics**: Inspired by the classic Tightrope physics—stay sharp to stay on the wire!
* **Global Leaderboard**: Compete with players worldwide.
